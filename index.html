Policies

  • 1. Order spot is only confirmed after checkout. Must be placed by end of the same day.
  • 2. Orders are only accepted Monday- Friday.
  • 3. 3-5 day processing time. Up to 7 days of processing time during sale and holiday periods.
  • 4. Order cannot be changed after order confirmation.
  • 5. No refund due to customer sizing errors. Sizing is the customer’s responsibility.
  • 6. Incorrect shipping address cannot be changed after the order is sent out.
  • 7. Kassaflora is not responsible for shipping carrier errors, once the order is scanned into the carriers system it is no longer Kassaflora's responsibility.
  • 8. No returns or exchanges.
  • 9. Standard order is 10 nails per set.
  • 10. Order must be purchased by end of the same day or the spot will be given to the next person in line.
  • 11. Pricing is dependent on design intricacies not length.
  • 12. No Wholesale orders
